Sheep in New Zealand

According to 2007 figures from the Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) of the United NAtions, there are about 39 million sheep in New Zealand. This means there are about 10 sheep per every human. Of course, those ratios are not equal across region. If you, understandably, fear sheep, and you want to know which regions to avoid in order to keep yourself safe, this website is for you.
